    About this app

    An app for soon-to-be parents faced with the taunting task of finding a unique yet recognizable name. Swipe through names, apply filters to match your preferences, and compare your matches.

    The data comes from Statistik Austria and covers every baby born in Austria since 1984. From 2010 onward, the dataset includes all births regardless of citizenship.

    Data is grabbed from Statistik Austria and reprocessed automatically once new data arrives. The full dataset and source code are available on GitHub.

    Have you found any issues or have a suggestion on how to improve? Open an issue or pull request!
